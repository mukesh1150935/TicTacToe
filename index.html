TIC TAC TOE

Tic Tak Toe is a very common game among children .It is helpful in developing kids
brain In which we used to play a lot of games.Its a very quick game ,not so time taking.

Tic-tac-toe is a fun game that you can play any time and anywhere as long as you
have a piece of paper, a pencil, and an opponent. Tic-tac-toe is a zero sum game,
which means that if both players are playing their best, then neither player will win.
However, if you learn how to play tic-tac-toe and master some simple strategies,
then you'll be able to not only play, but to win the majority of the time.

How to play

  • Choose your opponent
  • Make three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win
  • Watch your opponent and block them if they get 2 in a row
  • Try a harder mode to keep it interesting
  • After complete the game ,press reset button and play another game
